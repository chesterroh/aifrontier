# AI Frontier
> Bilingual podcast transcript site for AI Frontier with episode pages and chapter navigation.

AI Frontier publishes Korean and English transcripts for each episode and links to the official YouTube videos.

## Core
- [Korean Home](https://aifrontier.kr/ko): Latest episodes in Korean
- [English Home](https://aifrontier.kr/en): Latest episodes in English
- [Korean RSS](https://aifrontier.kr/ko/rss.xml): New episodes feed (KO)
- [English RSS](https://aifrontier.kr/en/rss.xml): New episodes feed (EN)

## Episodes
- [Korean Episodes](https://aifrontier.kr/ko): Episode list (KO)
- [English Episodes](https://aifrontier.kr/en): Episode list (EN)
